### Description of Assignment 1
<p>
i.	Students are required to prepare a VIDEO BLOG to UTMDigital Open Day.
ii.	The video contributes 10% of the course assessment and it is a group assignment. The video can be created using any related software and its content is based on visit to UTMDigital Open Day.
iii. Required content in the Video Blog:
      a)	Introduction of your group and the visit <br>
      b)	Overview of the Program/Visit <br>
      c)	Detail presentation on UTMDigital <br>
      d)	The attended talk <br>
      e)	Conclusion and Reflection <br>
</p>
